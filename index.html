About Me

Currently, I work as a Design Lead at Blizzard Entertainment, crafting tools and sites for video games in sunny, Southern California.

I manage a small group of designers within Battle.net across multiple product teams. Say hi!

Blizzard Battle.net Leading Platform Design Teams

Battle.net is Blizzard’s online gaming service, combining multiplayer connectivity, purchases, account management, social features, and more into a single package.

Currently, I lead several teams within “Battle.net & Online Products,” including a group tasked with making tools and tech decisions for Blizzard’s web properties.

Some early adopters of our tech are shown below, including Diablo Immortal (also below), Warcraft III Reforged, and World of Warcraft Classic.
